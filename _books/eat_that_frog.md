**Main Review**

The core principle of this book is to tackle your most difficult task (the "frog") first thing in the morning. This concept is central to overcoming procrastination and maximizing efficiency.

* **Key Takeaways:**
    * The 80/20 Rule (Pareto Principle) applied to tasks.
    * The importance of planning and clear goals.
    * Techniques for breaking down large, overwhelming tasks.
